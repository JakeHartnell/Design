We want to enable everyone to easily and cheaply grow things; for food.

What will you grow?


Our Mission:

To help usher in the next agricultural revolution through open knowledge and open technology.

Open source

Common Garden is interested in building ecosystems rather than platforms. Our products will help other businesses make money; after all we aren't going to do something ambitious as an Agricultural Revolution alone.

Standards based

We are using the latest and greatest in W3C backed web standards, meaning hardware makers will finally be able to focus on making awesome hardware rather than some dumb app.

Works with any device

If you are a third party hardware maker feel free to contact us. We'd love to hear about your needs.
